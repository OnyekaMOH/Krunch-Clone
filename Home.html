WELCOME TO KRUNCH

Krunch is about helping young people to discover who they are, what they want to be and how to get there.

Around the UK

KRUNCH

Krunch began in Oldbury, Sandwell and we have been supporting and mentoring local
children and young people for over 20 years. We provide bespoke one to one mentoring,
therapeutic emotional health and wellbeing interventions and alternative education
placements for 9-19 year olds.

SOUTHWEST

Krunch South West in Thornbury provide youth activities and mentoring for local young people.
