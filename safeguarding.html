Safeguarding

Krunch has a full raft of policies and practices to keep children safe and well, which are in line with the directives of Sandwell Childrens’ Safeguarding Partnership. Our Safeguarding Policy sets out the processes to prevent and protect children and young people from abuse and harm. Krunch is committed to provide appropriate and professional responses to any young person who has been abused or harmed. This support will reflect the individual child’s language, religious beliefs and any disability/difficulty the child may experience. Our staff are regularly trained and supervised to ensure that they act appropriately and professionally in the event of an abuse disclosure. Click here to read our Safeguarding Policy here.

The Safeguarding Lead Officer at Krunch is Lynsey Grant.

She can be contacted at Krunch Office on 0121 552 5556 or via email: Lynsey@krunch.org.uk
